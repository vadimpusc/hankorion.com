Something To Think About


  1. What is your film about?
  2. Is it a "sellable" idea?
  3. Are you going to write a script or improvise?
  4. Can you write a script? (It’s easier than it seems)
  5. Is your movie easy to shot? (You don’t want to rely on promises and favours)
  6. Can you film it under a week? (This will save money)
  7. What camera will you use to film it? (Use what’s available to you, or rent for short term)
  8. Can you film it yourself or will you need a crew? (This will reflect on final cost of the film)
  9. How can you record best sound possible?
  10. Can you edit and grade your film yourself?
  11. Can you do post-sound work? (Dialogue, mix etc)
  12. Where will you get your music from? (Composer or music library)
  13. What’s your distribution plan?
